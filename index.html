About Bexterart

I am Rebecca, a self-taught artist based in Sweden. On BexterArt, I showcase my collection of portraiture, digital art, and classic art. My aim is to inspire others through my unique approach, blending realism and emotion to create one-of-a-kind pieces for both personal and commercial projects.
